WELCOME TO ADIoT 2024

The 7th International Conference on Attacks and Defenses for Internet-of-Things (ADIoT 2024)

1. Conference Location:

ADIoT 2024 will be held at Zhejiang Pacific Hotel (1188 Zhijiang North Road, Haining, Zhejiang, China). More information can check: http://zhejiangpacifichotel.cn/.

2. Public Transportation to the hotel.

i) Hangzhou East Railway Station:
 Metro Line 1 from [East Railway Station], get off at [Yunshui Station Exit B], transfer to bus No. 3148 to [Shihe Road Lingtao Road Intersection Station], and walk 180 meters to the hotel
Hangzhou City Railway Station:
Metro Line 1 from [Cheng Station Exit A3], get off at [Yunshui Station Exit B], transfer to bus No. 3148 to [Shihe Road Lingtao Road Intersection Station], and walk 180 meters to the hotel

ii) Hangzhou Xiaoshan Airport:

Metro Line 1 from [Hangzhou Xiaoshan International Airport Station], get off at [Yunshui Station Exit B], transfer to bus No. 3148 to [Shihe Road Lingtao Road Intersection Station], and walk 180 meters to the hotel Hotel
Hangzhou Bus Passenger Transport Center:
Take Metro Line 1 from [Passenger Transport Center Station] to [Yunshui Station Exit B], transfer to bus No. 3148 to [Shihe Road Lingtao Road Intersection Station], and walk 180 meters to the hotel

iii) Self-driving:
The hotel has a total of 400 parking spaces. Self-driving vehicles of staying participants can be parked in the hotel garage for free. If they cannot park in the hotel garage, they can park in public parking spaces in nearby communities (charges are required according to the parking lot standards)
Metro Line 1 start and end time:
Xianghu-Xiaoshan International Airport (starting 05:54-end 22:50)
Xiaoshan International Airport-Xianghu (starting 06:00-end 22:30)
